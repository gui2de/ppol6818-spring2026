## Survey Description

Target population: Urban households in the DC–Maryland–Virginia (DMV) area who are responsible for or aware of their household electricity usage.

The survey is designed to understand household electricity use patterns, energy-saving behaviors, and attitudes toward energy conservation.

The questionnaire contains four modules:

1.  **Identification module** – Uses a server dataset with `search()` to select respondents and `pulldata()` to retrieve respondent information.
2.  **Demographics module** – Collects basic household characteristics such as housing type, household size, income category, education, and employment status.
3.  **Household energy use module** – Quantifies electricity-related behaviors, including electricity bills, appliance usage time, and heating or cooling usage.
4.  **Energy-saving behavior and attitudes module** – Measures respondents’ conservation behaviors and perceptions using Likert-scale questions.
