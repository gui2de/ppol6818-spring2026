<h1>Emilia Antúnez</h1>

<h3>Fritz Fellow in the Tech & Society program</h3> 

<h5>MPiP Co-President</h5>

<p>She is a policy analyst with experience in economic analysis, international affairs, and environmental projects. Her roles span from the Global Environment Facility, Mexico’s Secretariat of Finance and Secretariat of Economy, and private investment firm Prodensa. She is currently a Master of Public Policy candidate at Georgetown University’s McCourt School. Her research as a Fritz Fellow focuses on building digital tools to support sustainability transitions in food systems.</p>
