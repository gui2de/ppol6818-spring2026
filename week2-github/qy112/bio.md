### My work experience

I don’t have full-time work experience yet, but I’ve done several policy research/analysis internships with NGOs. Two of them focused on China’s low-carbon transformation in the steel and cement sectors, mapping the current landscape and technology pathways that are priority areas for the Chinese government. I’m currently working on the impacts of the EU’s carbon border adjustment mechanism (CBAM) on the green transformation of the global steel industry, such as the cost exposure different countries may face.
